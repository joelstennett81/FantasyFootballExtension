Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 25, 2025

Information We Collect

When you register or log in, we collect your email address and basic account details (first and last name, username). This information is used to create and manage your account.

When using the VORP Vision extension, no additional personal data is collected. The extension only fetches public fantasy football rankings data from our API at fantasyfootballextension.onrender.com.

How We Use Information

  • Email addresses are used for account authentication and login.
  • We may use your email to send important service-related updates.
  • No personal information is shared with the extension itself — only your authenticated session is used to retrieve rankings data.

Permissions

The extension requires limited permissions (activeTab, scripting, and host permissions for Sleeper and our API). These are used only to inject and display the rankings overlay panel on Sleeper’s site.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Account data is used strictly for providing access to the VORP Vision platform and extension.

Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect stored account information. However, no online system can guarantee 100% security.
