About VORP Vision

Draft the right player at the right time. This tool shows you how.

What is VORP?

Value Over Replacement Player (VORP) measures how much better a player is compared to the next replacement-level option at their position. It highlights where the biggest positional drop-offs happen, so you know which positions matter most in each round.

What is VOAS?

Value Over Average Starter (VOAS) compares a player’s projected points to what an average starter at their position would score. This shows who truly gives your lineup a weekly edge, not just who is better than a bench player.

Why it matters

Fantasy drafts are about opportunity cost. Taking a wide receiver early might mean missing the last elite running back. Waiting too long on tight end could leave you with a weekly gap. VORP and VOAS cut through the noise and reveal which positions are most valuable now versus later.

Use it during Sleeper drafts

VORP Vision isn’t just a website—you can also install the Chrome extension to use live during your Sleeper drafts.

  • Overlay: Rankings and VORP/VOAS values appear right on the Sleeper draft board.
  • Sources: Projections are blended from ESPN, CBS, and Draft Sharks for balanced accuracy.
  • Smart features: Drafted players auto-hide, position filters and tiers are built-in, and you can switch between VORP and VOAS views.
  • Personalized: Replacement level and average starter values adapt to your league size and roster setup.

My intent

I built this because I love the strategy of fantasy football. Rankings alone don’t tell the full story—context does. VORP Vision helps you see who is most important to draft each round so you can make sharper choices with confidence.
